Set up your game

How to play ▾

CyberParty is a gamified learning game. Move around the board, answer questions, and rack up points — the more you know, the higher you score.

🎲

Move & Answer

Roll the dice, move around the board, and answer the question on each tile. Every correct answer earns points.

⭐

Earn Points

Brand tiles give you points when you answer correctly. Higher-value tiles are worth more.

❓

Bonus & Penalty Tiles

Quiz, Luck and Challenge tiles give bonus points. Wrong answers and penalty tiles cost you points.

☁️

LMS System

Connects to your LMS: your quiz accuracy and final standing are reported automatically.

🏆

How to Win

The player with the most points after the set number of rounds wins.
